Chris Bache Archive

This archive preserves the public talks, interviews, and reflections of philosopher-author Christopher M. Bache, spanning 2014–2025. It includes transcripts, diarized transcripts, and original audio/video media, mirrored across GitHub, Zenodo, and the Internet Archive.
